About Alex Neal

My name is Alex Neal, and I am launching a specialized one to one soccer coaching service designed to give players the type of individual attention that is rarely available in traditional team environments. As an international college student athlete, I bring a unique perspective and a high level of experience to every training session. Before coming to the United States, I competed at a high level in England, representing England Schools and having multiple academy experiences, before playing 4 years of college soccer as well as some college coaching in my career. That experience developed not only my technical ability but also my understanding of elite training standards, professionalism, and leadership to get to the next level. Many young players today train primarily in team sessions, where coaches must focus on 15 to 20 players at once. While those environments are important, they rarely allow enough time for detailed technical development, position specific coaching, and individualized feedback. Cre8ive Soccer fills that gap.
